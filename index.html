Cleanix: today's solution to a brighter future.

Who are we?

Cleanix is an innovative solution allowing volunteers across the world to take care and clean the places they love. Our company is already present worldwide despite our recent creation. Don't miss the opportunity to be part of the team and come on board of an amazing human and environmental adventure. We just need your smile and strengh, Cleanix provides all the necessary material and organizes the cleaning sessions in collaboration with the town councils and partner companies!

Our founder

Our founder is a passionate student willing to inspire and be an actor of today's world. Convinced by the power of youth and the worldwide call for a better future, he launched Cleanix after a trip to Indonesia which was at the same time insightful and extremely worrying.
